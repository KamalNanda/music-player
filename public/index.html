Music Player

Pick a Song to Play!



    Voice Commands

    Command(s) Description
    Play/Resume Play or Resume the current song
    Stop/Close Stop playing the current song
    Pause Pause playing the current song
    Restart/Start Again Play the song from beginning
    Toggle Playback toggles the current play state of song
    Increase Volume/volume up/turn the volume up Increase the volume by 10% (upto 100%)
    Decrease volume/volume down/turn the volume down Decrease volume by 10%
    maximum volume/increase volume to maximum Set volume to 100%
    Mute/Minimum volume Set volume to 0%
    increase speed Increase playback speed by 1 step
    decrease speed Decrease playback speed by 1 step
    find and play 'song' Search for the song & play the first result
    Find 'song' Search for the song and display results
    Play song number 'x' Plays the given song 'number' from search results
    Play song number 'x' from playlist Plays the given song 'number' from playlist
